About

      
Introduction

      
Welcome to the Mining for Potatoes Server! When the server was started by QwertyCyclone, it was just a server for him and his friends to play on together. Now it is a whitelisted PvE server. We aim to create a community of Minecrafters who build and explore together. The server is run on Bukkit, but the only plugins we use are for anti-griefing purposes, so it is a complete survival experience for you the player. Another interesting feature for you to know is that admins, ops, and even the owner all play in survival and never cheat in items. 

	  
Server Information

	  
The server's difficulty is on hard mode. In our theory, that makes the game more challenging as well as fun. There are no "/back", "/home" or "/warp" commands on our server because that is not legit at all. Our main form of travel to other bases or builds is our Nether Hub. We have 2 tunnels running opposite from each other and paths to portals going out from the sides of them. We also have a brewing area, a smelting area, an enchantment exchange area, as well as community storage area.

	  
There is information about the guidelines on the rules page.
