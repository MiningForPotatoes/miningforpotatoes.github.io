Rules

      
Building Guidelines


  • No building within 500 blocks of spawn. That is communal space reserved for future building projects. If you would like to build in spawn town, ask a staff member if you're allowed to before you begin.
    • 

  • Your build or base must be connected to our nether hub (ask a staff member if you need help connecting your build).
    • 

  • It is highly suggested that your builds be group projects. Our goal is to have builds that other players can participate in. This is what really brings the community closer together.
