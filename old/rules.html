Rules

      
These rules are strictly followed on the Mining for Potatoes Server.

	  
	  
You must follow these rules. If a rule is broken, we will give you one (1) warning via email but if you continue to break rules we will ban you without warning. If you see a felow player breaking any rule, post a comment on this reddit post.

      
1. Don't grief anything. It may look like an accident to you, but it could be someone's masterpiece in the making.

	  
2. Don't steal from chests. We do not have locked chests on the server, but that isn't an open invitation to take from them without the explicit permission from the owner. We have plugins which monitor and log each and every chest on the server, so be warned.

	  
3. Take and give. We have a community storage area in our Nether hub. Take as many resources as you need, but replenish them as well. It doesn't always have to be 100 percent fair, but make sure you are giving and not just taking. That being said, dont trade 5 melons for a diamond.

	  
4. Don't cheat. No Nodus, no X-Ray, no fly-mod. The only mods acceptable on the server include minimaps, any mods which deal with changes the visual aspects of the game (optifine, shaders, etc.), and inventory/chest tweaks (sorting, etc.)

	  
5. Your builds must follow our building guidelines.

	  
6. We recommend that you join us in Mumble. Although it is not required, it helps us become a more close-knit server and enriches gameplay. Mumble information is in the sidebar.

	  
(To ensure that you've read this, choose the 2nd answer ("Yeah") on the "Have you read the rules?" question in your Whitelist Application)
