    About

    We created Mining for Potatoes about a year ago, but don't let our age fool you. We have members who have played with each other for much longer than that, each with their own quirks and fancies. From the owner, to the mods, to the players, since the beginning, we've aimed to provide the best goddamn server to play, hang out, and just have a blast of a time.

    Specs
