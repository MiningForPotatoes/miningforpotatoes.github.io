    Rules

    Even though we are usually very flexible when it comes to what you can and cannot do, there is still a small set of rules you must follow.

    Never, ever be rude to people.

    As mentioned before, we aim to create a good experience for the players. So that means no harrassing people, making fun of them, their skin, their builds, their country, their name, or their religion.

    No griefing and hacking.

    While this usually applies no matter what, we felt we should still state it here. Destroying other people's builds, contraptions and other things is not okay. Not only will this hurt the people who made the built, since they did all that hard work for nothing, but it will also create fuss and fights, which is something we don't want. If you don't grief and don't hack, then you're welcome!

    Do not steal.

    This speaks for itself. No stealing, under no circumstances. It is not allowed to go up to someone's base and go steal their diamonds or other valuables just so you can be a bit more rich. If you want to borrow/take something from their chests, we advise you ask them beforehand. To ensure that you've read this, choose the 2nd answer "Yeah" on the "Have you read the rules?" question in your Whitelist Application.

    Wanna apply?

    We're always on the lookout for bright new spirits to join our awesome community. So if you think you will fit in nicely, don't hesitate to apply!
